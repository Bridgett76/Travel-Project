Why Tokyo

A lively and futuristic metropolis

Tokyo is known for being a lively and futuristic metropolis. It features a unique mix of the old and modern that blends perfectly with ancient temples hidden among large, modern architecture. Over 14 million foreigners visit Tokyo each year. It has been a well-traveled destination for decades.

Cherry blossoms, Mt. Fuji, and sushi grace the postcards and photographs. There are lots of fun things to do in Tokyo, even for the little ones. Tokyo Tower was inspired by the Eiffel Tower. It is also home to the Tsukiji Fish Market, the largest fish market in the world.
